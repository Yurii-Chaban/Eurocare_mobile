EUROCARE

about us

EUROCARE is one of the first home care agencies operating in Chicago and North Shore founded in 1984 that understands the needs of aging population and began to provide quality home care for the elderly. We are bonded and licensed by the State of Illinois, Department of Public Health and Department of Labor. We carry bond and general liability insurance. All of our caregivers go through vigorous screening and background check. They also go through multi-phased elderly care training. Our caregivers will perform a whole variety of tasks including home care services, companionship services, and recovery services with special attention given to the needs of those who are undergoing rehabilitation after stroke, cardio vascular disease or orthopedic surgery. Our caregivers are properly trained to handle the needs of those who suffer from Alzheimer's and dementia as well as from diabetes.
